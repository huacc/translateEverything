current_translation:
Our high-quality, recurring earnings sources and active management of our in-force portfolio drove operating profit after tax growth of 5% to USD 5.942 billion, and maintained the operating profit margin at a stable 16.9%. Renewal premiums collected increased by 10%, with recurring premiums representing 90% or more of total premiums collected in 2020. The growth in operating profit after tax benefited from significantly positive claims experience throughout the year, which offset the decline in yields on new fixed income investments and the impact of reduced assumed long-term equity investment returns. As expected, the exceptional positive medical claims experience reported in the first half did not recur in the second half, but we continued to see positive contributions from this source to operating profit after tax growth. With AIA's Thailand business lapse experience returning to normal levels, the renewal rate improved in the second half, while the Group's renewal rate was 95%.
